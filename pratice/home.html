Welcome to Student Information System

This platform helps you register students, store their details and view the record in a simple and organised way.
It's designed for schools, departments and administrators who wants to manage students data efficiently.

Steps to follow:

  1. Go to the "Register" page and fill out the student details form.
  2. Submit form to save the record.
  3. visit the "view Students" page to see students database.
